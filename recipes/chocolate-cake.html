Chocolate Cake

Back to Recipe Index

Ingredients

  • 200g all-purpose flour
  • 200g sugar
  • 75g cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 240ml milk
  • 120ml vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 240ml boiling water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour two 9-inch round pans.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; mix until smooth.
  4. Stir in boiling water until the batter is thin.
  5. Pour batter into prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
