Chicken Curry

Back to Recipe Index

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger until softened.
  2. Add curry powder and cook for another minute.
  3. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and simmer until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with cilantro, and serve.
