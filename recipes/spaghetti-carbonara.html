Spaghetti Carbonara

Back to Recipe Index

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and grated Parmesan.
  4. Drain spaghetti and toss with pancetta and garlic.
  5. Remove from heat and mix in the egg mixture quickly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper and serve hot.
